How to get your Instagram export

  1. Open Instagram → go to your profile → tap the menu (☰).
  2. Go to Accounts Center (or Settings → Account → Download your data).
  3. Choose Your information and permissions → Download your information.
  4. Select Download or transfer information → pick your Instagram account.
  5. Choose Customize information; under Connections check Followers and following.
  6. Set date range to All time and Format to JSON. Submit the request.
  7. Instagram emails you a link when the export is ready (can take minutes to days).
  8. Download the ZIP and drop it here — or unzip first and drop the folder.

Privacy: This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data is uploaded or transmitted anywhere. The page works offline once loaded.
